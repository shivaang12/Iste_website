Welcome to ISTE GCET STUDENT CHAPTER


The Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE) is the leading National Professional non-profit making Society for the Technical Education System in our country with the motto of Career Development of Teachers and Personality Development of Students and overall development of our Technical Education System. ISTE GCET(G H Patel College of Engineering and Technology) is one of the many student organizations working under ISTE.
